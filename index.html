Your catering service

Denagoxo S.A. provides our customers with food service to a various of events, from small familiar events to weddings and VIP meetings.

Founded in 2002, Denagoxo S.A has rapidly escalated, becoming the top catering company at local and provincial level. Denagoxo S.A employs more than 500 employees around the Basque Country, Cantabria, Castille and a foreign office in Cardiff, Wales.




Our Services

Events

We offer a range of special services from public events to VIP cocktails. Our proposals are flexible and we can attend any necessity to make any event exclusive to our customers.

Take Away

For those who put work before its personal integrity, we have designed special packages of take away food to keep yourself healthy on your 20th hour of day work.

Micromarket

Not everything has to be big, our micromarket service provides small products to be carried directly home.
